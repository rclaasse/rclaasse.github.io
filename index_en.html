🩺 Primary care, travel medicine and acupuncture

I welcome each person into a space of attentive care and respectful attention, offering services in primary care, travel medicine and acupuncture.

The consultation is a moment of listening and comprehensive evaluation, where the symptom is understood within the context of daily life, personal history and psychophysical balance.

The goal is to build personalised therapeutic pathways, focused on prevention, symptom management and the improvement of quality of life.

🧘 Areas of care

Primary care — listening, clinical evaluation and management of the main health conditions, with personalised attention to the person’s history and needs.

Travel medicine — dedicated consultations before departure: prevention, vaccinations and health advice to travel safely and with peace of mind.

Acupuncture — targeted treatments for pain, tension and functional disorders, with a gentle and respectful approach to mind-body balance.
