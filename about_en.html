Dr. Giorgia Argentini

I am Giorgia Argentini, a Medical Doctor and Acupuncturist. My work focuses on primary care, travel medicine and acupuncture, offering comprehensive and personalised care.

I believe in a medical approach based on authentic listening, a relationship of trust and active collaboration with the patient. Each treatment pathway is built on a careful assessment of needs, resources and individual goals.

Book an appointment

My approach

Each person carries a unique clinical and personal history. For this reason, every consultation is a moment of global assessment and dialogue, aimed at understanding not only symptoms but also the factors influencing them.

  • Comprehensive evaluation (symptoms, lifestyle, goals, ongoing treatments)
  • Treatment plan shared, clear and progressive
  • Monitoring of progress and adaptation of the care pathway
“An effective care pathway arises from the combination of medical expertise, genuine listening and active patient involvement.”

Education and expertise

  • Medical Degree — University of Trieste
  • 20 years of international experience — primary care, public health and infectious diseases in Africa, South America and Europe
  • Travel medicine and infectious diseases — Master’s degree at the London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine and field experience (Ebola, Zika, COVID-19)
  • Diploma in Acupuncture and Traditional Chinese Medicine — AMAB School, Bologna (2024)
  • Clinical and institutional experience — IRCCS Burlo Garofolo, MSF, UWCAD, ICTP

Main areas

  • Primary care — symptom evaluation, management of common health conditions and guidance throughout the care pathway.
  • Travel medicine — personalised consultations before departure: prevention, vaccinations and health advice for safe travel.
  • Acupuncture — targeted treatments for pain, tension and functional disorders, with a gentle and personalised approach.
  • Personalised approach — each pathway is tailored to the individual’s needs, goals and personal history.
